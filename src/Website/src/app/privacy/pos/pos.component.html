Privacy Policy ‘WOM Point of Service’

Personal data collected for the following purposes and using the following services:
  • Authentication: password and username
  • Non-continuous geolocation: geographic location
WOM POS will ask for the following permissions during regular operation:
  • Location: using your device’s GPS to find current location (this permission is optional)
Communication protocols with the WOM POS online service have been developed in order to be privacy-preserving by design:
  • Unique identifiers of sessions and other sensitive data are never transmitted to the online service and are kept on the device;
  • No personal information is ever stored on our servers.
The service is compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation.
